# Data sets

The data sets can be downloaded from sociopatterns.org or from Drive(Antonio-Giulia)


## LH10.dat
Hospital interactions  
### Format 
> t , i , j - at time t interaction among node i and node j.   
### 5 Categories: 
> Admin  
> Etude  
> Med  
> Paramed  
> Patient
